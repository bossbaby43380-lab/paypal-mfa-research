# TA0001 — Initial Access

## Objective

Understand how PayPal establishes authenticated sessions.

---

## Activities

- Captured login requests
- Observed MFA challenge flow
- Tracked session cookies
- Monitored authentication redirects

---

## Observations

- Session identifiers appear before MFA completion
- MFA challenge generated after credential validation

---

## Security Relevance

Authentication state sequencing may become relevant to MFA bypass analysis.

---

## Status

No bypass identified.
Further validation required.
